Virtual Invest Meetup,
January 21, 2021

Invest Meetup is a great place to:

• find promising HealthTech and BioTech Startups to invest;

• meet VCs focused on Impact investment;

• learn from the Industry Experts.

The Meetup will start with the Panel Discussion "Fundraising for BioTech, HealthTech, LongevityTech Startups".

The second part will be dedicated to the Presentations of selected HealthTech, LongevityTech, and BioTech Startups.

Date and Time: January 21, 10 am - 1 pm PT | 1 pm - 4 pm ET

If you are Fundraising for HealthTech or BioTech Startup, please Apply, and we will get back to you.
